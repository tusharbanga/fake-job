‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I check a job posting URL directly?

Yes! Our tool can analyze both job descriptions that you paste and direct URLs to job postings on major job sites and company career pages.

Is my data secure when I use this tool?

Absolutely. We don't store any job descriptions or URLs you submit longer than needed for analysis. All data is encrypted and handled according to strict privacy standards.

What are common signs of a fake job posting?

Common signs include unrealistic salary offers, requests for upfront payments, vague job descriptions, poor grammar/spelling, personal email domains, and pressure to respond quickly.

What should I do if I've already applied to a scam job?

If you suspect you've applied to a scam, stop all communication immediately. If you've shared financial information, contact your bank. Consider reporting the scam to authorities and job boards.
